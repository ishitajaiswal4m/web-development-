About DSC KIET

Developer Student Club KIET is inspired by Google Developers Family. We started our journey in Feb 2019. We try to
engage student developers through our hack events, codelabs and meetups. The motive is to create a local
ecosystem of programmers & hackers in and around the Campus. The technology awareness is main goal for first few
years of the group.
